Built from the ground up and subjected to rigorous testing for the past ten years, iPaladin's smart blockchain technology connects the dots. It weaves together every sliver of communication threads, work items and documents within your office landscape. iPaladin serves as your advocate - your permanent archivist. It provides context to information with landmarks and road signs, exposing currently hidden information, leveraging the time and talents of people around the intelligence of your business. Once your dots are organized as knowledged, you extract intelligence. How? When you are ready, model a client-focused structure of your business, coordinate the team around required operations and information, assign the work to gain group consensus and get going. iPaladin eliminates pain points truggered by the needles blocking and tackling that's required to manually reassemble business knowledge.
